Welcome to Bangla Hub ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

📋 1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide (Signup)

When you create an account, you must provide:

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Identity Data First name, Last name Account identification
Contact Data Email address, Phone number Communication & email verification
Profile Data Profile photo (optional) Personalization & discovery

1.2 Guest Access (Browse Without Account - No Personal Data Collected)

Bangla Hub allows you to browse content without creating an account.

  • 👁️ You can view events, services, job postings, business listings, and educational content
  • 🔍 You can search and explore the app freely
  • 📍 Manual state selection is required - you must select a state from the list to enter the app (this is not GPS tracking)
  • 🚫 You CANNOT upload events, post jobs, create business listings, or add service provider profiles
  • We do not collect personal data from guest users. Basic technical data required for app functionality may be processed but is not stored or used for identification.

1.3 Signup Location (Tap "Use Current Location" - NO Permission Dialog)

During signup, you tap the "Use Current Location" button. No permission dialog appears at this time.

  • 📍 You tap "Use Current Location" on the signup screen
  • 🔐 NO location permission dialog appears during signup
  • 📊 Your location (city, state, country) is stored temporarily until email verification
  • ✅ You can complete signup without seeing any permission dialog

1.4 Location Permission Dialog (Appears AFTER Email Verification)

After you verify your email address, the location permission dialog appears. This is the FIRST time you see a permission request.

  • 📍 The location permission dialog appears after email verification, before state selection
  • ✅ You can Accept or Deny the permission request
  • 📊 If you Accept, your device GPS detects your current city, state, country, latitude, and longitude
  • ⚠️ If you Deny, you can still use the app with manual state selection (no GPS features)
  • ⚙️ You can change this permission anytime in device settings
  • We do not continuously track or store location data in the background. Location is only accessed when you actively use location features.

💡 Important - Permission Timing

Location permission dialog appears AFTER email verification, BEFORE state selection screen. Not during signup.

We do not continuously track or store location data in the background.

1.5 State Selection Screen (Appears AFTER Location Permission)

After you respond to the location permission dialog, the State Selection screen appears.

  • 🗺️ You must select a state from a list of all US states
  • ✅ You cannot access the home screen without selecting a state
  • 🔄 This selection acts as a global location filter - all screens show content relevant to your selected state
  • 📍 This is separate from GPS location - you select manually from a list
  • 🔘 Each screen has a Change Location button to update your selected state
  • 💾 Your selected state preference is stored in your account

1.6 Post Location from Map (REQUIRED for Publishing Content)

When you post any content on Bangla Hub (events, job postings, business promotions, service listings, etc.), you must select a location from the map picker.

  • 🗺️ You select a location by picking a point on the map or searching for an address
  • 📍 We collect the address, latitude, and longitude of your selected location
  • ✅ This is required for all posts - you cannot publish content without selecting a location
  • 📌 The location you select is displayed publicly on your post
  • ✏️ You can choose any location - it does not need to be your current location
  • 🔐 NO additional GPS permission is needed for the map picker - you can search for any address

1.7 Content You Create and Post (Registered Users Only)

When you use our interactive features as a registered user, you may provide:

  • 📅 Event listings (title, description, date, location from map, images)
  • 💼 Job postings (company name, job title, requirements, salary, location from map)
  • 🏪 Business promotions (business name, description, products, offers, location from map)
  • 🔧 Service provider listings (service category, pricing, availability, location from map)
  • 📚 Education services (tutoring, admissions guidance, Bangla classes, location from map)
  • 🤝 Business partner requests (partner type, budget, requirements, location from map)
  • ⭐ Reviews and ratings for services
  • ❤️ Likes and interests on events and listings

Note: Bangla Hub does NOT have a save/bookmark feature. You can like events, but cannot save them for later.

1.8 Images and Photos

Bangla Hub allows registered users to upload images for:

  • Profile photos - Stored securely with Cloudinary
  • Event banners and images - Stored securely with Cloudinary
  • Business logos and gallery images - Stored as base64 in Firestore
  • Service provider profile images - Stored as base64 in Firestore

🎯 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Create and manage your user account
  • Email Verification: Confirm your identity after signup
  • Location Permission: Request GPS access after email verification
  • State Selection: Store your manually selected state for content filtering
  • Post Location: Store map locations for your posts (address, coordinates)
  • Signup Location: Temporarily store your location from "Use Current Location" tap
  • GPS Location (Optional): If you accept permission, GPS detects your current location
  • Event Sharing: Share events via device sharing options
  • Service Delivery: Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Communication: Send account notifications via email
  • Support: Respond to your inquiries
  • Security: Prevent fraud and spam
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws

Note: Bangla Hub does NOT use any analytics services. We do not track user behavior or collect usage statistics.

⚖️ 3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • User Consent: When you create an account and agree to our Privacy Policy
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the services you request (event posting, job listings, etc.)
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services, prevent fraud, and ensure platform security
  • Legal Compliance: When required by applicable laws and regulations

For location data: Processed based on your explicit consent when you accept the location permission dialog after email verification. We do not continuously track or store location data in the background.

For state selection: User-provided preference data stored in your account.

For post location: Required for publishing content - map picker does not require GPS permission.

🔒 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • 🔐 Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • 🔑 Secure Firebase authentication with email/password
  • 🛡️ Firestore security rules to prevent unauthorized access
  • 🔄 Regular security updates and monitoring
  • 📁 Secure image storage with Cloudinary
  • 🔢 Base64 encoding for Firestore-stored images

⚠️ Important Note

While we implement strong security measures, no system can be completely secure. We continuously work to improve our security practices and protect your data.

🗑️ 7. Account Deletion and Data Retention

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Account: You can delete your account anytime via Settings → Delete Account
  • Data Removal: Upon deletion, we remove all your personal data immediately including:
    • Profile information (name, email, phone, photo)
    • Your signup location (city, state, country, coordinates)
    • Your selected state preference
    • Your posted events, job postings, and business listings (including their map locations)
    • Your service provider profiles
    • Your likes and interested events
    • Your authentication credentials
    • Your images stored on Cloudinary
  • Public Content: Content shared publicly may remain visible to other users who have previously accessed it.

🗑️ Data Deletion

When you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently removed from our servers immediately. No backups are retained.

  • If you don't receive the email, you can request a new verification link
