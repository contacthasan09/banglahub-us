1.2 Location Data

We collect precise location data (latitude/longitude) to:

  • 📍 Show relevant events, services, and businesses near you
  • 📍 Enable location-based search for tutors, service providers, and business partners
  • 📍 Allow you to post events with accurate location information
  • 📍 Provide city and state-specific content recommendations

💡 Location Permission

Location data is only collected when you grant permission and is used solely to provide location-based features within the app. You can disable location services in your device settings at any time. However, some features like "nearby events" and "local services" may be limited without location access.

  • 🔧 Service provider listings (service category, pricing, availability)
  • 📚 Education services (tutoring, admissions guidance, Bangla classes)
  • 🤝 Business partner requests (partner type, budget, requirements)
  • ⭐ Reviews and ratings for services
  • ❤️ Likes and interests on events and listings

We automatically collect certain information when you use our app:

  • 📱 Device information (model, OS version, unique device identifiers)

  • 🌐 IP address and network information

🔒 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • 🔐 Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • 🔑 Secure Firebase authentication with email/password
  • 🛡️ Firestore security rules to prevent unauthorized access
  • 🔄 Regular security updates and monitoring
  • 📁 Secure storage and access controls for user-uploaded content

⚠️ Important Note

While we implement strong security measures, no system can be completely secure. We continuously work to improve our security practices and protect your data.

🗑️ 6. Account Deletion and Data Retention

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Account: You can delete your account anytime via Settings → Delete Account
  • Data Removal: Upon deletion, we remove all your personal data within 30 days including:
    • Profile information (name, email, phone, photo)
    • Your posted events, job postings, and business listings
    • Your service provider profiles
    • Your likes and interested events
    • Your authentication credentials
  • Backup Retention: Some data may remain in encrypted backups for up to 90 days
  • Public Content: Content you shared publicly may remain visible if others have saved it

👶 7. Children's Privacy

Bangla Hub is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately. For users aged 13-18, we recommend parental supervision.
